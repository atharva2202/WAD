Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Department

The Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Department offers programs in electronics, communication, and related fields. Students learn about analog and digital electronics, communication systems, signal processing, and embedded systems. Graduates find employment in industries such as telecommunications, electronics manufacturing, and embedded systems development.
