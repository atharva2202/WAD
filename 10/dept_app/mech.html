Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department offers programs in mechanical engineering and related fields. Students learn about thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and mechanical design. Graduates find employment in industries such as automotive, aerospace, energy, and manufacturing.
